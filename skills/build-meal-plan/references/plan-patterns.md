# Plan Patterns

## One-Day Plan

Use three meals and one optional snack. Balance calorie categories across meals unless the user asks for a strict target.

## Weekly Meal Prep

Prefer recipes with:

- Clear refrigerator guidance.
- Reheating guidance.
- Ingredients that keep well.
- Larger serving counts.

## Bulking Plan

Favor:

- Bulking Friendly or High Calorie category.
- Higher protein per portion.
- Higher carb sides when training is mentioned.

## Low-Calorie Plan

Favor:

- Low Calorie category.
- High protein where available.
- Low sugar and high fiber when relevant.

## Family Plan

Favor:

- Family Friendly group.
- Larger serving counts.
- Simple cooking steps.
- Common equipment.
